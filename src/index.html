About Brazos Valley Robotics

Brazos Valley Robotics BEST Robotics team for Homeschooled students and students attending smaller Christian schools in grades 6-12 in the Bryan/College Station, TX area.

Established in 2022 with 18 studentswe’re a fun energetic group that loves laughter, good times, and can’t miss out on making a good joke! Thank you to all the generous people and businesses who have greatly helped our team in many different ways, we really appreciate it! We also would like to thank BEST robotics for this fun learning opportunity and for all the supplies they’ve provided!
